U.S.S. Planet Express - Futurama

The U.S.S. Planet Express is the ship from the animated, satirical show, Futurama. It is a delivery ship for the company Planet Express, and it carries mail across the universe. The ship and its crew constantly are endangering themselves through incompetence, idiocy, and for plot.

Facts About the Ship
  • Designer - Professor Hubery Farnsworth
  • Notable Crew - Philip J. Fry, Turanga Leela, Bender, Doctor John A. Zoidberg, Amy Wong, Hermes Conrad
  • Speed - 99% of the new light speed
  • Engine - Dark matter reactor, providing 200% fuel efficiency, by not moving the ship, but the universe around it.
  • Weapons - Laser cannon and 2 torpdeo tubes
  • Pressure Resistance - Anywhere between 0 and 1 atmosphere
